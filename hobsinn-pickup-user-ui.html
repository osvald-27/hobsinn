ℹ️ What is a Special Call?
An urgent, on-demand pickup request
A Special Call happens when a household needs a pickup outside the normal weekly slots — for example, after a party, a move, or an unexpected amount of waste. The user pays a higher per-bag rate because it's urgent and unscheduled.

When a Special Call comes in, you get a notification. You can accept or decline — there's no penalty for saying no. If you accept, you agree to go within the time window the user requested.
📖 Real example
Amina hosted a birthday on Friday night. Saturday morning she has 7 extra bags. She opens HOBSINN, books a Special Call for 10 AM. You get notified, accept, arrive, collect 7 bags → 7 × 600 XAF = 4 200 XAF sent to your MoMo when done.
